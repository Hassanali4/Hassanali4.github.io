Project information

  • Genre: 3D , Driver Simulation
  • Player Experience: Skeptic,Interactive
  • Project date: 8 February,2023
  • Tools Used: Unity(C#)
  • Play here: itch.io

Main Objective

You are given a sports car which is a little hard to handle But still contorllable to explore the City by driving it..

What I did

  • Designed and developed the core functionalities such as making the car controller to move smoothly as possible using Rigidbody & Wheel Colliders.
  • Implemented the smoke particles to work when the break is applied to the car with the condition of speed above 10.
  • Implemented a singleton pattern for the soundtrack player,Scene persists,level manager.
  • Designed and developed skid marks for the car when its breaks are applied but only from 2 tires behind it using Trail Renderer component.
  • Created a system for lights for front & Back of the car.
  • Contructed a toggle system for the fron lights of the car & added a UI button with lights icon on the top right corner of the Screen.
  • Added a Info button that enables a Pannel with little info graphics that how can the player be able to use the car in scene.
  • Added a car sound that changes its pitch according to the speed of the car by getting the rigidbodys velocity & magnitude to give a real car like feel using sound.
  • Contructed a stable camera system for both cars which works differently for the day & night scene cars using the state cams of Cinemachine in Unity.
  • Contructed the overall level design of the game.
  • Worked on playtesting and overall balancing of the game.
