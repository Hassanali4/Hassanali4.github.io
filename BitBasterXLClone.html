Project information

  • Genre:2D,Arcade, Endless,2D Shooter,Sci-fi,Retro
  • Player Experience: Intense
  • Project date: September 24,2022
  • Tools Used: Unity(C#)
  • Source Code: Github
  • Play here: itch.io

Main Objective

Control your Rocket by steering left & right to avoid the enemies & eliminating them with your desired powerup also collect supplies on the way so that your rocket doesn't get runout of fire power. The simple bullets are infinite like the enemies so that you may have a fair fight against a never-ending horde of space monsters, achieve the highest score, and good luck Captain.

What I did

  • Designed and developed the core player functionalities such as movement,shooting and health.
  • Constructed enemy AI to move in the scene along the player if player touches them his health will be decreased.
  • Developed a set of Power Ups like a Shield, Multi-Shot & Laser that when achieved by the playe will be enabled for 5 seconds.
  • Constructed a health point system for enemy as well as player.
  • Designed and developed efficient ammo system.
  • Designed and developed several pickups such as ammo , health , powerups & Bomb.
  • Implemented a bomb feature with a UI button if the player use it all the enemies in the scene will be cleared giving a breaze to the plater from continouse wave of enemies for better experience.
  • Developed UI system for ammo,health and player control.
  • Developed a simple level manager for handling different menus.
  • Constructed a music feel mechanism for better player experience & give juice to the game experience.
  • Constructed an overall level design of the game.
  • Worked on playtesting and overall balancing of the game.
