Main Objective

Challenge your mind in my Number Guessing game! Guess the magical numbers shown on the console and level up to the next challenge. Be careful not to add the wrong input or you'll hear a warning sound. With randomly generated numbers and a classic design, this game is sure to test your skills and keep you engaged. Developed in Unity with a focus on UI and programming, this project showcases my technical skills and attention to detail.

What I did

  • Designed and developed the core player functionalities, including input handling, to create a smooth and responsive gameplay experience that engages players and keeps them coming back for more.
  • Developed an efficient random number generation system to create a varied and challenging gameplay experience that keeps players engaged and interested.
  • Created a user-friendly UI system for the player input field, streamlining the gameplay experience and allowing players to focus on the action and excitement of the game.
  • Constructed a music feel mechanism that enhances the player experience and adds to the overall excitement and intensity of the game, creating a more immersive and engaging experience for players.
  • Designed an overall level design that provides a challenging but fair gameplay experience, incorporating a variety of difficulty levels and randomly generated numbers to keep players engaged and interested.
  • Conducted extensive playtesting and balancing to ensure that the game is challenging but enjoyable, keeping players engaged and coming back for more while avoiding frustration or boredom.
